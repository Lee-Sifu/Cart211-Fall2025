üõ°Ô∏è Privacy Organizations Fighting Back

Electronic Frontier Foundation (EFF)

Website: eff.org

What they do: Leading nonprofit defending digital privacy, free speech, and innovation. Fight surveillance, provide privacy tools, educate public, challenge bad laws in court.

Notable work: Created Privacy Badger, fought against government backdoors, sued over mass surveillance.

Privacy International

Website: privacyinternational.org

What they do: Global organization investigating surveillance systems, challenging companies and governments, advocating for privacy rights worldwide.

Focus areas: Data exploitation, surveillance technology, human rights.

Access Now

Website: accessnow.org

What they do: Defend and extend digital rights globally. Provide 24/7 digital security helpline, fight internet shutdowns, advocate for policy change.

Impact: Helped secure GDPR passage, fight surveillance laws globally.
