The Real-Time Bidding Auction

What Happens in 100 Milliseconds When You Load a Webpage:

0-20ms: Your browser loads the page. Trackers identify you via cookies and fingerprinting.
20-40ms: Your data profile (age, location, interests, browsing history, purchase intent) is sent to ad exchanges.
40-70ms: Thousands of advertisers bid on you in a real-time auction. Highest bidder wins access to your eyeballs.
70-100ms: Winning ad is selected and loaded. You see the ad. Platform gets paid.

This happens 5 trillion times per year. You are auctioned off constantly.
